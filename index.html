Welcome!

This is a tool for 2nd person inquiry or automating coaching processes. There are many ways you can use it!

2nd Person inquiry is commonly used in psychology as a means for facilitating a sythesis between aspects of your psyche. It is also used for facilitating shadow work and cultivating intimacy with the essential.

Learn More

For Shadow Work, or Shadow Integration Work (via Jung, Freud, etc.)

For facilitating the "Big Mind Process" (via Genpo Roshi)

For facilitating "Journey Work" (via Brandon Bays)

For facilitating trauma healing or conflict resolution with someone who is not available to be present with you (e.g. healing from conflict in a relationship wherein someone has passed)

For healing issue with money (Some brilliant 'Money Coaches' will have you have a daily 'talk with money' to make sure you and your internalized sense of money are on the same team, working together, etc.)

Extending the ideas and patterns in the above approaches, this tool can be used to address almost any issue in your life... to some extent... to procure fresh perspective on almost any matter...

I will add more information, specific guidance and maps/systems for many of these lineages and applications, but I want to know what is useful for you.

A Personal Introduction (w/Video)

Tony Robbins once said something along the lines that you can achieve anything if you focus all of your resources, brilliance, attention and will towards it. I felt inspiration and doubt both in considering this view.


"What if I wanted to fly?"

This was my first thought. I cannot fly. While in a lucid dream, this is my first choice, but in waking life, I cannot fly.

Today, as I write these words, I am in awe in recognition that I was wrong.


I can fly.


Last week I was sharing drinks with friends (a very rare occassion), and someone busted out the NYTimes article "To fall in love with anyone, do this". I started reading the questions and immediately said "Lets all answer these!".


One of the questions was "What is your idea of a perfect day"?


While I desperately wanted to say it was the actualization of my fleeting taste of a worldcentric creative impulse; (how I could give my unique gift to humanity and leave a legacy which uplifts the world), what was most alive for me was this: "I want to jump out of an airplane in a bat-suit and fly with my soulmate by my side".


There was WAY more vitality and aliveness in me as I expressed this then I had for my desire to leave a legacy. I felt shame in recognizing this, because my experience was so different then my intent, and my whole being yearns deeply for integrity with its chosen trajectory. For me, this was evidence of my lack of moral developement, as I aspire to have my gift for the world be the most vital dedication of my life that I can express.


As I reflected this to a friend she suggested that the two might actually complement each other. I shuddered with recognition, and the Koan "what is the sound of one hand clapping" came to mind. Of course! The intersection of these two aspirations: the yearnings of my self for the ultimate experience of freedom and the yearnings of my essential nature to give my deepest gift... one hand clapping does not make a sound, but when two hands clap something emerges... a sound which is not possible with each independently.


How could my passionate yearning to fly be complementary to my desire to connect intimately with my worldcentric creative impulse? My desire to give my gift fully to you and the world in a way that grows beyond me, beyond the scope of my life, uplifting humanity at large in perpetuity?


All this said, this project is the closest possible expression I can must for how I might leave my legacy. I aim to stand on the shoulders of giants, those humans who have taught humanity unique approaches to recognizing, uncovering and actualizing latent potential in distinct ways. On one hand I want to automate those processes so that you can learn them experientially, but on another hand I want to apply everything I have studied to date via Integral Theory in order to reveal, through your experience, how these tools complement each other. And, ideally, I want to provide you an experiential taste of all of this. While the theoretical foundations can be nourishing to the mind, I find that life experience is what has fundamentally transformed the way I see, experience and live my life even more so then the ways that I have learned to see.


Each of the tools I present to you has a theoretical foundation, and over time I will add links so that when you find something that moves you to the core, you can investigate the foundations which have given form to these practices.


My promise to you in this moment is this: I will show you how to turn all of the resources that I have found relevant in overcoming obstacles to actualizing human potential towards one particular aim at a time, such that you can more effortlessly focalize your attention and creative powers to actualizing any dream you set out to dream.


I will stop there, however, as I am going to also let you take full creative freedom in HOW you go about engaging the universe you contain within as you set out to dream, to actualize your dreams, and to become a self reflexive human being.


I am going to show you how to apply all of these tools to your intentions, your visions, your dreams, to that which inspires you, to what you appreciate about being alive, to the questions you choose to sit with as you move through life, to the insights that emerge through being with the questions, to the goals you set for yourself, to the habits you choose to engage and commit to along the path to living your aspirations and actualizing your goals, to the challenges you face in confronting your fears and participating more fully in being alive, to the decisions you must make that cause you to take pause and engage your capacity for discernment and even to the projections that your mind makes up about yourself and other people which provide a rabbit hole into insight about your most direct path to becoming a more whole, more integrated, more congruent and more coherent expression of the very humanity you hold which makes explicit your interconnection with all other humans alive in this tremendous moment of our worlds history.


This is the minimum of what I am going to give you in my life, and this app stands already as evidence that it is possible. Going beyond this, I am unwilling to be satisfied by the idea of coaching you in a an automated fashion through technology. Its simply not enough. I am using this app's practices on myself every day and it is, pure and simple, not enough. I am going to teach you as best I possibly can how to coach yourself, how to customize your practice, how to integrate it so that I become obsolete, so that this app becomes obsolete, so that even opening your computer becomes obsolete, by crafting a practice with you, for you, in your style, that turns the new nueral pathways we create together into nueral highways, which makes your practice an autopilot practice.


If this was enough perhaps I would be satisfied, but unfortunately even this is not enough to have me feel as though I am fully giving my gift. So, I am going to take this at least one step further. At the right moment, I am going to show you how to bring these practices and ways of relating into every conversation you choose. I want you to have the choice, at any moment, in any relationship, to allow the subtle interconnectivity between you and another be made explicit. I want you to grow into a radical receptivity of the reflection your relating offers, so that by relating alone, that which was obscure about your being becomes highlighted in a safe, gentle fashion, such that you can even more so allow yourself to soften into your own essential freedom, harmony, safety, brilliance, generosity, compassion, insight and humanity.


This may be enough to satiate my desire to give my gift... but I don't want you to take my word for it, that its possible for you to dream big and actually succeed at actualizing your dreams, so I am going to show you its possible. Today, with $5 in the bank and $2 in my pocket, I am choosing to fly. I want you to watch me make this dream happen, and as you see me moving towards its actualization, I want you to let yourself dream a little bit bigger. Every step I take towards flying, I want you to take a step towards dreaming bigger. And when I take flight, I want you to step up to the plate and take flight in your dreams.

Process whats here

This tool contains processes designed forge new nueral patterns in your mind, translating a novel journaling experience into automatic responses to what is, ushering in resiliance, competence, emotional intelligence and a more adaptive self, and to address most of the types of circumstances one would seek personal development practice around... to systemetize and integrate some of the worlds most relevant practices, and to make them so customizeable, that your process of discovering your own unique path to actualizing the latent potential of your unique calling is unavoidable!

The Sedona Method

The Sedona Method Resistance Clearing Process will guide you through a cycle of noticing what is present in your experience, welcoming it as it is, identifying any resistance to this, welcoming that as well, and then letting go of the desire that what is be any different then it is.



The Sedona Method Decision maker tool will guide you through considering different options for yourself, identifying unconscious motivational factors at play, and then working with those now-conscious motivators intentionally via a process similar to the above.

Simple Journaling combined with the Clearing Process can reveal layer after layer of our inner worlds which would otherwise remain in the auspices of the unconscious mind.

The Immunity to Change Coaching Method

The Immunity to Change Coaching Method is designed to help you discover unconscious motivators inhibiting making behavioral changes you may otherwise aspire to accomplish.



This is in its infancy, but may be actually very useful as it is!. I hope to build out and take this particular tool alot further... it is the brainchild of Robert Kegan at Harvard.

2nd Person Inquiry

2nd Person Inquiry is an overarching concept which enfolds Shadow Integration (or psycho-synthesis), Gestalt and many other practices.

The actual application of 2nd Person inquiry draws from a wide range of approaches and is probably best understood through the examples.

Anytime you have a conversation with your anger, or your inspiration, and speak on behalf of yourself and your anger, switching perspectives at each sentence, for example, your in a 2nd person inquiry.

Simply asking yourself questions and then answering from a unique vantage point is another. This will likely make more sense in practice.

Inspiration and Influences

There are many influences which inspire this particular tool, as it is designed to be an expression of Applied Integral Theory; Ken Wilber's Body of work, in that it is moving towards being a sactuary for the part of the mind which is unwilling to close its eyes to the radically revealing nature of inclusivity in regards to perspectives, or vantage points, on the nature of our own existance.

In other words, its here for minds unwilling to close, or at least curious about the potential that unique perspectives have to complement each other.

For those to whom this makes no sense, it will still serve as a platform for the process of self-discover and the actualization of the unique brilliance and potential we each hold... and eventually, the ways in which this tool demonstrates, by example, the complementary nature of otherwise apparently disparate ways of seeing, will soften the exclusivity claim of our diverse vantage points on existance; making space for a wider embrace of each other, of diversity... a wider sense of inclusivity and honor for the differences we each bring to the world.


Ken Wilber

Ken Wilber's Applied Integral Theory is probably the single most significant influence on this project, as indicated in the summary above

The Enneagram

While the Sedona Method brings a clear map to oriented towards and relating to unconscious motivators and influences, the Enneagram expands that map with a detailed summation of patterns which tend to run unsconsciously, motivating human behavior and obscuring the fullness of our capacity to be aware.

This tool pulls from these patterns in much the same way as it would via the Sedona Method Patterns, and enables our choice around how we want to relate to them. This models the pattern of psychosythesis, or shadow integration from Jungian Psychology.

Compassionate Communication

One of the original influences to this work! NVC or compassionate communication teaches individuals to recognize the connections between emotion and circumstance, between longings/values and emotions, and to reveal these connections as an invitation to others to create together an experience of deeper connection or to evoke a behavioral shift.

I struggled when a group agreed they could not 'feel' me, and I realized I wanted to be more connected to and aware of my emotional state... which was one of the first reasons I started building this app.. which now reveals over time the emotional texture underlying my experiences, the values underlying those emotional states and more.

Avatar

The Avatar Lineage of self inquiry, which I do not recall many details of, does teach a practice they call 'releasing fixed attention,' and which I have lovingly begun calling 'looping'.

Looping can be used to address trauma or patterns that are deeply fixed... it may be helpful for addressing some of the deeper ways in which we are stuck or fixed in outdated patterns. More to come.

The Mankind Project

Just one practice so far is inspired by the Mankind Project; the 'wanting for' practice, which is designed to be applied to a relationship where struggle exists, to cultivate clarity, self authorship and compassion. More info to come

There are only a couple things the app is designed to remeber at present, your top 3 goals, for example, and your dedication of your life, the next 3 months or your practice. More will be included over time, but you will be notified about it as it happens.

Are there any keyboard shortcuts?

Just Control + S so far, which opens and closes the experience tab
